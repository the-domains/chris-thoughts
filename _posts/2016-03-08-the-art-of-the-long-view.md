# The Art of the Long View

by Peter Schwartz
![](https://the-grid-user-content.s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/9f340202-a2c9-481f-98f5-6614674cc787.jpg)

Great book about scenario planning. It will help you develop a strategic vision within your business. The approach has been [adopted by Shell][0] a long time ago. 

It makes an interesting pair with "The Fifth Discipline".
